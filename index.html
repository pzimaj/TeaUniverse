Welcome!

Our teashop/cafe was created in order to connect all tea lovers in one place. It is a place where a lot of different people can connect and talk while drinking their favorite cup of tea. Unfortunately, do to the COVID-19 sutuation, Tea Universe can't let customers come into the shop. In order to give our customers the change to drink our tea even during the pandemic, we have decided to create a webpage where the customers can order their favorite tea to their address. We have 3 different types of tea: Green, Fruit and Herbal. Each type of tea has specific flavors made to compliment the type of tea. Green tea flavors are: sencha, matcha, and sakura cherry tree. Fruit tea flavors are: Rosehip, Strawberry and Vanilla, and Caramel Apple. Herbal tea flavors are: Chamomille, Mint, and Hibiscus. Each tea flavor has their own special message that can come with it. Each flavor has 2 special famous life quotes that come from famous sources. After selecting the type of tea, the flavor of that tea and the quote to go with it, the order will be sent to us after the user submits the form that is located at the bottom of the page.
